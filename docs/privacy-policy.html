VibeText Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 31, 2025
Developer: D3Marco, LLC
Contact: nickd@demarconet.com

1. Introduction

VibeText ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our iOS app collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our voice messaging service integrated with Apple's Messages app.

Key Privacy Principle: VibeText processes your voice recordings locally on your device. We never store, transmit, or have access to your actual voice recordings. Only transcribed text is processed through our AI service to provide tone adjustments.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Voice Recordings

  • What: VibeText accesses your device's microphone to record voice messages
  • How: Voice recordings are processed entirely on your device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework
  • Storage: Voice recordings are NOT stored, transmitted, or retained by VibeText
  • Purpose: Voice recordings are converted to text locally and then immediately discarded

2.2 Transcribed Text

  • What: Text transcriptions of your voice recordings
  • Processing: Transcribed text is sent to OpenAI's API for tone adjustment and enhancement
  • Retention: Text is processed ephemerally and not stored by VibeText
  • Identity: Text processing is not linked to your personal identity or device

2.3 Technical Information

  • App usage analytics (crashes, performance metrics)
  • Device compatibility information for support purposes
  • No personal identifiers or location data

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Functionality

  • Voice-to-Text Conversion: Converting your voice recordings to text using on-device processing
  • AI Enhancement: Sending transcribed text to OpenAI for tone adjustment and message refinement
  • Message Delivery: Facilitating the sending of your enhanced messages through Apple Messages

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyzing app performance and reliability
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Enhancing user experience based on usage patterns

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 OpenAI API

Service: OpenAI's GPT API for text processing and tone adjustment

Data Shared: Only transcribed text (never voice recordings)

Processing: Ephemeral processing without data retention

Privacy Policy: OpenAI Privacy Policy

4.2 Apple Services

  • Speech Recognition: On-device transcription via Apple's Speech framework
  • Messages Integration: Message delivery through Apple's Messages app
  • App Store: App distribution and updates through Apple's App Store

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

  • User preferences and settings stored locally on your device
  • No personal data stored on our servers
  • All data protected by iOS security measures

5.2 No Remote Storage

Important: VibeText does not maintain user accounts, databases, or cloud storage. Your privacy is protected by design through local-only processing.

5.3 Security Measures

  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No persistent user identifiers or tracking
  • Minimal data collection principle

6. Permissions

6.1 Microphone Access

Purpose: Required for voice recording functionality

Control: You can revoke microphone access in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone > VibeText

Impact: Revoking microphone access will disable voice recording features

6.2 Messages Integration

Purpose: Required for iMessage extension functionality

Access: Integration allows message composition and sending

Control: Managed through iOS Messages app settings

7. Your Rights

7.1 Data Control

  • No Data Collection: Since we don't store personal data, there's no data to access, modify, or delete
  • Processing Transparency: All processing is disclosed in this policy
  • Opt-Out: Simply delete the app to stop all data processing

7.2 Communication Preferences

  • No marketing communications from VibeText
  • Support communications only upon user request
  • Updates delivered through App Store notifications

8. Children's Privacy

VibeText is suitable for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at nickd@demarconet.com.

9. International Users

VibeText processes data in accordance with applicable privacy laws. OpenAI's services may process data in various jurisdictions as described in their privacy policy. By using VibeText, you consent to this processing.

  • Updates to this webpage
